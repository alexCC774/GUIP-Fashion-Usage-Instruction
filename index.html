How to use

Charge your wallet

Before you use your wallet tracking function, please fully charge the wallet via wireless charging devices like wireless power bank or wireless charge set.

Connect with your phone

If your phone is IOS system, you can directly connect with your wallet in Bluetooth function.

If your phone is Android System, you can scan the QR code behind this usage instruction card to download an application to connect with your wallet.

Active tracking function of your wallet

Pressing the button of your wallet to activate the tracking function of your wallet. And you can see the tracking information on your phone

Caution

  • Do not touch the device with wet hands.
  • Do not disassemble, modify or repair the device.
  • Before using your wallet, please fully charge it.
  • Keep your wallet dry and away from moisture and corrosive materials.
